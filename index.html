Our Story

W O R B S Beauty is a collective mission born in Ghana. We believe beauty shouldn't be a luxury, but a shared standard of care. Our community is our compass, guiding every formula we develop to ensure YOU are never an afterthought in your beauty journey.

Our philosophy is Clean. Intentional. Essential. We strip away the noise to focus on high-depth, sustainable ingredients that respect both your skin and our planet.

The WB Loop: Our Circular Commitment

We are honest about our footprint. Return your used WB tubes to us, and we'll reward your commitment with 3% cashback on your next purchase. These materials are then diverted to certified plastic processing partners to offset our primary footprint.

Our paper-base & biodegradable bags ensure every order respects the planet as much as it respects your skin. This is more than lip care, it is a sustainable ritual built on transparency and trust.

Shipping Information

We deliver across Ghana with care and speed. All orders are packaged in our signature eco-conscious bags to keep your products safe and our planet happier.

Delivery Days

W O R B S delivers on Wednesdays and Fridays every week. Orders placed before midnight on Monday will be dispatched on Wednesday. Orders placed before midnight on Wednesday will be dispatched on Friday.

💡 Want to ensure Wednesday delivery? Place your order by Monday at midnight. For Friday delivery, order by Wednesday midnight.

Delivery Areas & Fees

  • Greater Accra (Accra, Tema, Madina, Adenta, Kasoa) — GH₵ 20–35 depending on zone
  • Kumasi — GH₵ 50 flat rate, dispatched Fridays only
  • Cape Coast — GH₵ 50 flat rate, dispatched Fridays only
  • Takoradi — GH₵ 55 flat rate, dispatched Fridays only
  • Other regions — Please contact us on WhatsApp for a quote before ordering

Order Confirmation

Once you complete your order via WhatsApp, our team will confirm your order, total amount (including delivery), and payment details within a few hours during business hours (Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm GMT).

You will receive a dispatch notification on the day your order ships.

Packaging

All W O R B S orders are shipped in biodegradable and paper-base bags as part of our commitment to sustainability. Your products will be nestled securely inside. If you're sending a gift, let us know via WhatsApp and we'll add a personalised note at no extra charge.

Returns Policy

Your satisfaction is everything to us. If something isn't right with your order, we want to make it right. Please read our returns policy carefully before reaching out.

What We Accept

Due to the nature of beauty and personal care products, we are only able to accept returns or exchanges in the following circumstances:

  • The product arrived damaged or broken during transit
  • You received the wrong product or shade
  • The product is defective (e.g. separated formula, broken applicator)
⚠️ We are unable to accept returns on products that have been opened and used, unless the product is faulty. Please choose your shades carefully — our team is always happy to help you decide via WhatsApp before you order.

How to Initiate a Return

  • Contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order via WhatsApp (+233 245 877 865) or email (worbsbeauty@gmail.com)
  • Include your order details, a clear photo of the issue, and your preferred resolution (replacement or refund)
  • Our team will review and respond within 1–2 business days
  • Approved returns must be sent back within 5 days of approval in their original packaging

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days via the original payment method. Delivery fees are non-refundable unless the error was ours.

The WB Loop — Tube Returns

This is different from a standard return! When you've finished a W O R B S product, you can return the empty tube to us and receive 3% cashback on your next purchase. This is our circular commitment to the planet. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange a WB Loop drop-off or pick-up.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you've been wondering about W O R B S — answered honestly. If your question isn't here, we're always just a WhatsApp away.

Products

  • Are your products safe for sensitive skin? — Yes! Our formulas are tested and free from parabens, sulfates, and harsh dyes. That said, if you have a known allergy, please check the ingredient list for your specific product or ask us.
  • Are W O R B S products cruelty-free? — Absolutely. We do not test on animals and never will.
  • How long do the glosses last on the lips? — Typically 1–2 hours with normal wear. Layering over WB Lip Butta can extend wear. Eating and drinking will reduce longevity (as with any lip product).
  • What's the shelf life of your products? — 12 months unopened, 6 months after opening. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Can I layer ROGUE over ROSÉ? — Yes! Layering our glosses is encouraged. Start with a lighter shade as base and build depth with ROGUE on top for a custom ombré effect.

Ordering

  • How do I place an order? — Add items to your bag on this website, then complete your order via WhatsApp. Our team will confirm availability, total, and payment details with you directly.
  • What payment methods do you accept? — We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money) and bank transfer. Payment details are shared once your order is confirmed on WhatsApp.
  • Can I order a personalised gift set? — Yes! The Sweet Talk Trio Set already comes with a personalised touch. For custom bundles or gift notes, message us on WhatsApp before ordering.
  • Do you offer discounts for bulk orders? — Yes — bundle orders of 3 or more items receive a 5% discount automatically. Larger bulk or wholesale inquiries? Contact us directly.

Delivery

  • When do you deliver? — We deliver on Wednesdays and Fridays across Ghana.
  • Do you deliver outside Accra? — Yes, to select cities including Kumasi, Cape Coast, and Takoradi. See our Shipping Info page for details.
  • Can I pick up my order? — Yes, in-person pick-up in Accra is available. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange.

Sustainability

  • What is the WB Loop? — Our circular return programme. Return your empty W O R B S tubes and earn 3% cashback on your next order. Returned tubes are sent to certified plastic processors.
  • Is your packaging eco-friendly? — Yes. We use paper-base bags, biodegradable mailers, and minimal plastic wherever possible.
